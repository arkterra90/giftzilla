Welcome to GIFTZILLA - Your Hub for Group Wish List Distribution!

How Does GIFTZILLA Work?
  • Someone in your group creates a registry and becomes your group admin.
  • Your group admin can then share your group's PIN with future group members to join the registry.
  • New group members should create a GIFTZILLA account and join their registry.
  • Once everyone has joined and completed their wish lists, the admin will be able to send a randomized and anonymous wish list to each member of the group.
  • If you're a group member, you'll receive an email with the wish list of another member for you to purchase them gifts.
  • Make sure to pay special attention to any notes your admin makes, so you have the details of when the gift exchange will happen.
  • Enjoy!
